## Domain definitions

### distribution
- **Scope.** Channel identity and distribution economics: brokers, coverholders,
  MGAs, binding authorities, delegated-authority bordereaux, line-slips.
- **Boundary.** Broker identity and brokerage/commission percentages live here.
  Bound premium does not, even on a bordereau (→ pricing/finance).
- **Warehouse artefacts.** `*_summary_brokerage`, `do_commission_details`,
  columns `commission`, `expiring_commission_percentage`,
  `london_order_percentage`, `london_estimated_signed_line` (placement share).
  Almost no coverholder / binder / MGA evidence in the sample — this book is
  either direct-broker or the delegated stack sits in another system.

### underwriting
- **Scope.** Risk selection and bind lifecycle; policyholder / insured identity
  and risk characteristics; coverage terms and line size.
- **Boundary.** Quote *identity, workflow, coverage terms, underwriter
  commentary* → here. Quote *priced outputs* → pricing. Quote *bound policy
  servicing* → operations.
- **Warehouse artefacts.** `*_quote`, `*_quote_setup`, `*_quote_policy_detail`,
  `*_quote_coverage_detail`, `do_entity_attributes`, `core_policy_view`,
  columns `uw_adjustment`, `uw_comment`, `policy_is_quoted`, `new_renewal`,
  `coverage`, `limit`, `excess`, `deductible`, `inception_date`, `expiry_date`.

### pricing
- **Scope.** Technical rate-making, rate adequacy, rate change, loadings,
  ELR / burn rate, benchmarking.
- **Boundary.** `tech_*`, `modtech_*`, `rate_*`, `elr`, `rarc` when used for
  *adequacy* → pricing. `gwp/nwp/earned` accounting views → finance.
- **Warehouse artefacts.** `*_layer_pricing`, `*_rating_factors`,
  `*_rating_details`, `*_risk_code_elr`, `*_pricing_pol_elrs_adequacy`,
  `hx_fi_adjusted_premium`, columns `tech_ggwp/gnwp`, `modtech_ggwp/gnwp`,
  `tech_gg_elr`, `modtech_gg_elr`, `gross_rarc`, `net_rarc`, `base_rate`,
  `rate_change`, `rate_index`, `loss_cost_inflation`, `claims_inflation`,
  `burn_rate_*`, `gglr_*`, `gnlr_*`, `trend_index`, `dev_pattern` (as rating
  input), `input_premium`.

### exposure_management
- **Scope.** Exposure measurement, aggregation, cat/PML, zonal concentration.
- **Boundary.** Pre-event "what's at stake" → here. Post-event loss → claims.
  Drift of exposure over time → portfolio monitoring.
- **Warehouse artefacts.** `*_exposure`, `*_exposure_model`,
  `*_hl_exposure_premium`, `*_claims_exposure`, `contingency_location`,
  `contingency_catastrophe`, `*_RegionalExposureView`, columns
  `exposure`, `exposure_measure`, `exposure_type`, `gross_exposure_change`,
  `country`, `main_domicile`. **Signal is thinner than expected** — no obvious
  PML / return-period artefacts in the sample.

### operations
- **Scope.** In-force servicing: endorsements, MTAs, cancellations, renewal
  processing.
- **Boundary.** Renewal *decisions* → underwriting; renewal *processing* → here.
- **Warehouse artefacts.** `hx_operational_detail`, and MTA / endorsement
  tables that **do not appear in the sample**. This domain is a placeholder —
  the warehouse may simply not expose servicing data yet.

### claims
- **Scope.** FNOL → closure: notifications, case reserves, paid, indemnity/
  expense split, adjuster activity, subrogation.
- **Boundary.** `case_reserve`, `paid`, `incurred` → claims. `ibnr`,
  `ultimate_loss` (actuarial) → reserving. Loss-development triangles used
  for pricing → pricing.
- **Warehouse artefacts.** `*_claims_experience`, `*_claims_details`,
  `*_claims_activity`, `do_entity_claims_history`, `eupi_specified_loss`,
  `hx_fi_claims_activity`, `ClaimLayersView`, columns `incurred`, `paid`,
  `gglr_incurred`, `gnlr_incurred`. **Caveat:** much of what looks like
  "claims" here is *claims experience used as pricing input*, not live claims
  ops — classify carefully (see Open questions).

### reserving
- **Scope.** Actuarial IBNR, ultimate loss estimation, LDFs, BF / CL,
  held-vs-indicated.
- **Warehouse artefacts.** No clear reserving assets in the sample. Columns
  `gglr_ultimate`, `gnlr_ultimate`, `burn_rate_ultimate` are reserving-adjacent
  but appear in *pricing* contexts (ultimate-based burn rates feeding rate
  adequacy). Placeholder domain.

### reinsurance
- **Scope.** Inward/outward treaties, facultative, retro, recoveries, treaty
  economics.
- **Warehouse artefacts.** Nothing explicit. Columns `our_share_pct*` and
  `london_order_percentage` are about co-insurance/placement share rather
  than cession. Placeholder domain — may be that reinsurance data sits in
  a separate ceded-re system.

### finance_profitability  *(renamed from `profitability`)*
- **Scope.** Accounting/P&L view: GWP/NWP, earned/unearned, DAC, combined /
  loss / expense ratio for *result attribution*, technical result,
  sold-to-plan, target-to-plan.
- **Boundary.** `sold_ggwp`, `sold_gnwp`, `target_to_plan`, `sold_to_*` in a
  plan/attribution context → here. Same measures used for rate monitoring
  → pricing.
- **Warehouse artefacts.** `profitability_measures`,
  `profitability_measures_total_our_share_usd`,
  `hx_ra_financial_metrics`, `hx_ra_financial_scoring`, `hx_ra_financials`,
  `hx_do_financials`, `hx_fi_premium_summary`, columns `sold_ggwp`,
  `sold_gnwp`, `sold_ggwp_our_share`, `target_to_plan`, `target_gg_elr`,
  `target_gn_elr`, `sold_to_tech`, `sold_to_modtech`, `gross_premium`.

### portfolio_monitoring
- **Scope.** Cross-cutting roll-up over time: KPI drift, concentration,
  rate-change trending.
- **Boundary.** `*_monitoring`, `*_dashboard`, `*_drift`, expiring-vs-new
  comparisons at book level → here. Individual-risk pricing/claims/finance
  → their own value-chain domain.
- **Warehouse artefacts.** `rate_monitoring`,
  `rate_monitoring_total_our_share_usd`, `ll_rate_monitoring`,
  `hx_rate_monitoring*`, `*_expiring_*` assets and columns, columns
  `change_in_sold_to_*`, `breadth_of_cover_change`,
  `gross_limits_and_excess_change`, `policy_term_change`, `other_changes`.
  **Boundary call:** the `expiring_*` columns are arguably underwriting
  (prior-term context during quote work) or monitoring (drift); see Open
  questions.

### compliance_regulatory
- **Scope.** Sanctions, conduct, regulatory returns (SII, IFRS 17, QRTs),
  GDPR.
- **Warehouse artefacts.** None visible in the sample. Placeholder domain.
